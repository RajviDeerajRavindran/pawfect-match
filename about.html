About Pawfect Match

Pawfect Match is dedicated to connecting loving humans with pets in need. While people can adopt dogs and cats, our primary focus is rescuing stray dogs across India. We provide a platform where every pet has a chance for a loving home, and communities are safer and more compassionate. Using our lightweight JSON-based backend and modern deployment practices, we ensure a smooth, accessible, and interactive experience for all users.
